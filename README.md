# Final_Project: Indigo
______________________________________________________________________________________________________________________
Indigo is a simple 3D game where you try to escape a dungeon that a ghost calls home.
______________________________________________________________________________________________________________________
Controls:
- W/A/S/D for player movement
- Space to jump
- Shift to run
- E to collect items, talk to Indigo, and interact with objects.
- Right click to attack
______________________________________________________________________________________________________________________
Objective: Complete the quests and escape the dungeon. Avoid the dangerous puddles and attacks from the enemy.

Gameplay Systems:
- Simple combat system with one enemy, triggered by an event
- Quest system for progress towards goal
- NPC dialogue and interaction
- Closed off area
- Player health system
- Environmental hazards
- Inventory to show collected items

Known Issues:
- Ingredients don't disappear after being used
- NPC interaction feels a little stiff as the ghost does not move to look at the player
- Ambience seems quieter than expected after battle music ends
